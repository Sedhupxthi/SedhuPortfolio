About me

Hi, I’m Sedhupathi Rajendran, a data enthusiast from Tamil Nadu, currently finishing my BTech in Computer Science and Engineering with a specialization in Data Science at VIT Vellore.

My journey into data began with a simple curiosity: "How can numbers tell a story?" Over time, that curiosity turned into a passion. I started exploring datasets, building projects, and learning how data can drive real decisions — from predicting sales to understanding customer behavior.

I’ve worked on projects ranging from CT kidney image classification using CNNs to building anti-spoofing and deepfake detection systems with MobileNet and ResNeXt. Recently, I’ve been diving deep into business data, building models to predict customer responses and optimize marketing efforts.

What excites me most is solving real-world problems with clean code, clear insights, and meaningful visualizations. I enjoy working with tools like Python, SQL, Tableau, and Excel, and I’m always up for a challenge that pushes me to learn and grow.

When I’m not working with data, you’ll find me exploring tech trends, tweaking dashboards, or trying to simplify complex concepts through storytelling.
